Phoenix Forge Technologies 🔥
A high-end GregTech expansion featuring Phoenix-enhanced multiblocks, exotic material synthesis, and advanced energy systems.



🌟 About the Project
Phoenix Forge Technologies is an endgame-focused GregTech expansion that introduces new multiblocks, exotic resource synthesis, and high-energy power systems. This mod builds upon the GregTech Community Pack, pushing the limits of technology, automation, and material refinement.

🚀 Features
✅ Advanced Multiblocks – Structures for resource generation, power production, and exotic matter synthesis.
✅ Endgame Material Progression – Utilize Phoenix Enriched Naquadah, Neutronium, and Akashic Zeronium for powerful components.
✅ Quantum & Dimensional Engineering – Tap into zero-point energy, reality condensers, and plasma fusion.
✅ High-Energy Fusion Reactors – Generate massive power through Singularity Fusion, Hyperfusion, and Infinitum Reactors.
✅ Balanced Progression – Designed for a structured LUV to MAX-tier technology journey.

Reality Condenser (UEV-Tier) – Produces Ultra-Dense Exotic Matter.

🛠️ 4️⃣ Unstable PhoenixWare Machines (ZPM - UEV Tier)
